2. Ginger Chicken Stir-fry

üçú Asian ‚è±Ô∏è 30 Mins üî• Dinner

Ingredients

  • 300g Chicken breast (cubed)
  • 1 Red bell pepper, julienned
  • 1 Green bell pepper, julienned
  • 1 Carrot, thinly sliced
  • 100g Broccoli florets
  • 2 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp Fresh ginger, grated

For the Sauce:

  • 3 tbsp Soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp Honey or brown sugar
  • 1 tsp Sesame oil
  • 1 tsp Cornstarch

Instructions

  1. Mix the sauce ingredients in a small bowl and set aside.
  2. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a large pan or wok over high heat. Add chicken and cook for 5-7 minutes. Remove and set aside.
  3. Add garlic and ginger to the pan, saut√© for 30 seconds.
  4. Add all vegetables and stir-fry for 5 minutes until slightly tender-crisp.
  5. Return the chicken to the pan. Pour the prepared sauce over the ingredients and stir for 1-2 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  6. Serve hot with rice or noodles.
