3. Fluffy Buttermilk Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups All-purpose flour
  • 3.5 tsp Baking powder
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tbsp Granulated sugar
  • 1.25 cups Buttermilk (or milk + 1 tbsp vinegar)
  • 1 Egg
  • 3 tbsp Melted butter

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Slowly add the wet mixture to the dry mixture and stir until just combined. Do not overmix (lumps are okay!).
  4. Lightly grease a non-stick pan and heat over medium heat.
  5. Pour batter onto the hot pan. When bubbles start to form on the surface and the edges look set, flip the pancake.
  6. Cook until golden brown on the other side.
  7. Serve hot with butter and maple syrup.
