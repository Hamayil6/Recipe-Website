5. 5-Minute Homemade Hummus

Ingredients

  • 1 can (400g) Cooked chickpeas, drained (reserve the liquid)
  • 1/2 cup Tahini
  • 3 tbsp Fresh lemon juice
  • 2 cloves Garlic
  • 1/4 cup Olive oil (+ extra for serving)
  • 1/2 tsp Cumin
  • Salt and Pepper (to taste)

Instructions

  1. Put the tahini and lemon juice in a food processor or blender. Process for 1 minute‚Äîthis makes the tahini creamier.
  2. Add the garlic and half of the chickpeas to the mixture and process.
  3. Add the remaining chickpeas, cumin, salt, and pepper. While processing, slowly add 2-4 tablespoons of the reserved chickpea liquid (aquafaba). Adjust for desired consistency.
  4. Continue blending until smooth and creamy.
  5. Transfer to a serving dish, drizzle with olive oil, sprinkle with paprika, and serve with pita bread or vegetables.
